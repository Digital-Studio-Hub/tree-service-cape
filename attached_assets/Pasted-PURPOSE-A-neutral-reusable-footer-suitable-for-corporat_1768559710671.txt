PURPOSE
A neutral, reusable footer suitable for corporate, informational, portfolio, nonprofit, or service websites. Emphasis on clarity, accessibility, trust, and maintainability.

CONTENT STRUCTURE

BRAND SUMMARY

Logo (SVG preferred)

One sentence description of the organization or website purpose

Optional tagline

NAVIGATION
Organized into clear groups:

Pages: Home, About, Services, Blog, Contact

Resources: Help, FAQ, Documentation, Support

Legal: Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Cookie Policy

CONTACT INFORMATION

Email address

Phone number (optional)

City and country (no precise address unless required)

SOCIAL LINKS

Icons only (SVG)

Open in new tab

Accessible labels

LEGAL & META

Copyright notice with dynamic year

Rights statement

Optional disclaimer line

ACCESSIBILITY & TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

Semantic HTML5

Keyboard navigable

WCAG 2.1 AA contrast

Mobile responsive

No inline styles

Lightweight and framework agnostic
